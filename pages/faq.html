Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

General

What dental services do you offer?

We offer a full range of dental services including routine check-ups, professional cleaning, teeth whitening, fillings, root canals, orthodontics, and cosmetic dentistry. Our team is equipped to handle both general and specialized dental care.

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM, and Saturday, 9:00 AM – 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Do you accept new patients?

Yes, we warmly welcome new patients of all ages. You can register online through our sign-up page or call us directly to schedule your first appointment.

Appointments

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online via our website, call our reception desk, or visit us in person. Online booking is available 24/7 through your patient portal.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card (if applicable), a list of any medications you are currently taking, and any previous dental records or X-rays if available.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask that you give at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations or missed appointments without notice may incur a small fee.

Treatments

Is dental treatment painful?

Patient comfort is our top priority. Most treatments are performed under local anaesthetic so you feel little to no discomfort. Our team will always explain each step and check in with you throughout the procedure.

How long does a routine check-up take?

A standard check-up and clean typically takes between 45 minutes and one hour. More complex assessments or treatments may require a longer appointment.

Do you offer teeth whitening?

Yes, we offer both in-clinic and take-home professional whitening treatments. Results vary by individual, but most patients see a noticeable improvement after a single session.

Insurance & Payment

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major dental insurance plans. Please contact our reception team with your provider details so we can confirm coverage before your appointment.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for larger treatments. We can discuss options during your consultation to find an arrangement that suits your budget.
